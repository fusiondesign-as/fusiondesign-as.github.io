BENEFITS OF FACEBOOK ADS

  • 10th June 2022
  • admin
  • General, Media

As a business owner, there are many benefits to using Facebook ads. Similar to other ads on social media, Facebook ads can help you grow your audience and increase brand awareness.

Here are the top three benefits of Facebook advertising:

1. Acquisition of New Customers

Facebook ads are great for acquiring new customers because they target your ideal audience very precisely. You can reach people who have a genuine interest in your product or service, which is great for conversion rates.

2. Building Better Brand Awareness

Facebook advertising also helps build better brand awareness by exposing your business to more people through targeted ads. People who have never heard of you will see your ads and may even become interested in learning more about what you offer. This way, they’ll remember your company when they need it most (when they go online to make a purchase). The more people that know about your business, the more likely it is that someone will purchase something from you eventually!

Including that list helps customers because it’s like your computer is your personal shopper, doing your shopping for you—or at least finding similar items which might spark your interest, based on past buying behaviour. Or the adverts might remind you that you looked at a few items but did not checkout your shopping cart selections. In this way, businesses reach back to you to try to convert the sale.

3. A Boost in Sales Revenue

In addition to building brand awareness, Facebook ads can increase sales revenue by driving traffic to specific landing pages on your website or mobile app where people can make purchases immediately after viewing an ad. In fact, some advertisers have reported seeing increases as high as 300% when they ran campaigns on Facebook compared with other platforms.
